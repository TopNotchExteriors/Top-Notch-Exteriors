Frequently Asked Questions

Clear information regarding project schedules, estimation values, and material lifecycles.

What premium roofing materials do you offer?

We source architectural asphalt shingles, modern standing seam metal roofing, and high-performance rubberized membranes (EPDM) engineered specifically for longevity, durability, and luxury visual aesthetics.

How long does a standard residential roof replacement take?

Most luxury residential roofing replacements are finished within 1 to 2 days, depending closely on scope, layout complexity, and weather conditions. We emphasize immaculate safety and thorough clean-up throughout the build phase.

Are you fully insured and registered to operate in Pennsylvania?

Yes, Top Notch Exteriors is fully insured and registered as a dedicated exterior construction firm servicing residential and commercial property assets across Elizabethtown, PA, and surrounding regions.
